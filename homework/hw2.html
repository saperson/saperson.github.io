TESLA CYBERTRUCK INSPIRES COMPETITORS
RIPOFFS TAKE TO STREETS
By Dave Jernalyst

Several Tesla Cybertruck ripoffs have taken to the streets in the wake of the company's susprise announcement on November 21st. Aiming to capitalize on the response to the truck's unique form factor, other automakers such as Dodge (pictured left) have announced their own takes on the Cybertruck's eye-searing style.
