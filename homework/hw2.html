TESLA CYBERTRUCK INSPIRES COMPETITORS
RIPOFFS TAKE TO STREETS
By Dave Jernalyst

Several alternatives to the Tesla Cybertruck have taken to the streets in the wake of the company's susprise announcement on November 21st. Aiming to capitalize on the response to the truck's unique form factor, other automakers such as Dodge (pictured left) have announced their own takes on the Cybertruck's eye-searing style.

Several automakers have already launched alternatives to Tesla's Model S sedan and Model X SUV, and these various Cybertruck competitors are only the latest in a common trend since the Model S's debut in 2012.
